Rock My World

Submitted by mgoh on Thu, 2011-03-03 14:53
March 23, 2002 to May 11, 2002

Marking the fiftieth anniversary of the term "rock 'n' roll", Rock My World: Recent Art and the Memory of Rock 'n' Roll surveys new art that draws on rock music as a critical reference point. Going beyond distinctions of high and low culture, these artists probe the myriad connections between rock, contemporary art, and other cultural arenas.

Featured artists are Jessica Bronson, Martin Creed, Jeremy Deller, Sam Durant, Rodney Graham, Evan Holloway, Christian Marclay, Erik Parker, Dario Robleto, Marina Rosenfeld, Steven Shearer, Frances Stark, and Mungo Thomson. The exhibition is free and open to the public.
