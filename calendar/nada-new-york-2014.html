Wattis Limited Editions Program
Friday, May 9, 2014 - 2:00pm to Sunday, May 11, 2014 - 5:00pm
Pier 36 - Basketball City, 299 South Street, New York

We are pleased to announce our second participation in NADA art fair, this time around in the New York edition, where we will present a curated display of our limited editions.

Over the past five years, the Wattis has been inviting artists whose work has been presented in its exhibitions to create an exclusive limited edition and help support the institution’s ambitious program. The high caliber of the Wattis Institute’s limited editions program is a testament to the strength and relationship to some of the most internationally recognized artists working today, and allows art patrons to own artworks by these artists in an accessible way.

For NADA New York 2014, we will present a selection of our most recent limited editions, organized around the idea of repetition. Featured artists include: John Baldessari, Walead Beshty, Ryan Gander, Claire Fontaine, Paulina Olowska, Laura Owens, Ed Ruscha, Collier Schorr, Jordan Wolfson.

The Wattis will be located in the main area, booth 609.

For more information about NADA, please visit www.newartdealers.org
